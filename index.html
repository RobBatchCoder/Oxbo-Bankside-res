MODERN RESTAURANT
IN LONDON’S BANKSIDE

Located between the Southbank and Borough Market, a stone’s throw away from the Tate Modern, Spacious and modern, the setting is perfect for romantic date nights, dining with friends and celebratory catch ups.

The menu is packed with popular flavours from around the world. Enjoy an OXBO signature dish such as the Aberdeen Angus Burger with homemade chips. Polish it off with a decadent dessert before heading into the restaurant’s bar, The Distillery, for after-dinner drinks.
